UK First-Time Buyer Price Analysis, 2011–2026

Local Authority District price trends with CPIH-adjusted real-terms growth and loss statistics.

Headline (real terms, CPIH-deflated)

  • UK nominal FTB price is up +72% since 2012 — but only +18.7% in real terms once 47% CPIH inflation is stripped out.
  • 20 of 349 LADs saw real FTB prices fall over the full 2012–2026 period. City of Aberdeen is worst at −48% real.
  • In the latest 12 months to Jan 2026, 221 of 349 LADs fell in real terms (median −0.8%). Nominal stagnation is real decline almost everywhere.
  • Genuine real-terms winners over 14 years: Salford +56%, Manchester +51%, Waltham Forest +50%, Oldham +46%, Tameside +44%.
